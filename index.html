CivilizationCraft

A fun, yet challenging Minecraft experience. For the player who wants to be apart of something greater. 

Our Philosophy

We strive to create a server experience that is both fun, immersive, and enjoyable, while also being challenging, hardcore, and intense. 

For all intense and purposes this server is a "real life simulation", aiming to be as realistic as possible, through the lenses of ancient history. 

We try to give players freedom to bend the world, and history of it, to their whim. 

Server Lore

You are in the midst of history, as humanity has started going from purely nomadic, to settling down, building cities and starting trade. 

Towns are starting to pop up, and history is beginning to be written. You must survive the harsh environment, famine, sickness, and war. 

As the world progresses, and civilizations rise and fall, where will you be during it all?
